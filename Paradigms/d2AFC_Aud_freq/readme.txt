d2AFC_Aud_freq: delayed two-alternative forced choice, w/ auditory stimulus, different frequencies
for example, low frequency go left lickport, high frequency go right lickport.
ref: Inagaki, et al. (2018). "Low-Dimensional and Monotonic Preparatory Activity in Mouse Anterior Lateral Motor Cortex." The Journal of Neuroscience 38(17): 4163.
